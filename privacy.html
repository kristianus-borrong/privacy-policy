Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 03, 2025

Introduction

Kumalaku Attendance respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data, including the face data used for attendance purposes.

Data We Collect

  • Identity Data: Name, email, phone number, and other account information you provide.
  • Attendance Data: Date, time, and location of your check-in/out records.
  • Face Data: - We do not store face data as photos or images.
    - Your face is converted into a digital code (string) and stored securely in the company’s internal database.

Purpose of Face Data Usage

  • To verify that attendance is performed by the real user and not through a photo or other media.
  • To help HR departments validate employee presence accurately.

Face Data Retention

  • Face data is only used for attendance validation.
  • Face data is stored for a maximum of 6 months from the last time it was used.
  • After 6 months, the face data will be automatically deleted from our private VPS server.
  • We do not store face data permanently or indefinitely.

Sharing of Face Data

  • We do not share your face data with any third parties.
  • All face data is processed and stored only on the company’s internal server.

Data Security

We apply technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, please note that no method of digital storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We remain committed to keeping your data safe.

Your Rights

  • You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • You may request the deletion of your face data before the 6-month period if desired.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised whenever changes are made.
